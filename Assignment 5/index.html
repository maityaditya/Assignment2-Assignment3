Doberman

The Doberman Pinscher is a sleek, muscular breed known for its intelligence, loyalty, and protective nature. Originating in Germany, Dobermans are excellent guard dogs and devoted family pets with a striking appearance and smooth coat.

Labrador Retriever

The Labrador Retriever is a friendly and intelligent breed with a short, dense coat in black, yellow, or chocolate. Known for their versatility and gentle temperament, Labs excel as family pets, service dogs, and working companions.

Bulldog

The Bulldog is a distinctive breed with a muscular build, wrinkled face, and pushed-in nose. Known for their gentle and affectionate nature, Bulldogs are calm and good with families and children. They are friendly and adaptable.
